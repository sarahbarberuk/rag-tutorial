# Financing Policy

PC Emporium offers financing through Affirm on orders of $200 or more. Customers can choose to spread the cost over 3, 6, or 12 months.

0% APR financing is available on 3-month plans for eligible customers. 6 and 12 month plans carry an APR of between 10% and 30% depending on the customer's credit profile.

Affirm financing is available to US residents only and is subject to a soft credit check which does not affect the customer's credit score.

Financing is not available on gift cards or store credit.
