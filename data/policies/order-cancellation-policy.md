# Order Cancellation Policy

Orders can be cancelled free of charge at any time before they are dispatched. To cancel an order, contact support@pcemporium.com as soon as possible with your order number.

If the order has already been dispatched, it cannot be cancelled but can be returned under the standard returns policy once delivered.

Custom-built PCs cannot be cancelled once the build process has begun, which is typically within 2 business days of the order being placed.
