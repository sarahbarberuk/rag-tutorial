# Repair & Servicing Policy

PC Emporium offers a paid repair and servicing service for PCs and components purchased from our store. Repair requests can be submitted by emailing support@pcemporium.com.

A diagnostics fee of $49 applies to all repair requests. If the customer chooses to proceed with the repair, the diagnostics fee is waived and only the cost of parts and labour is charged. Estimated repair costs will be provided before any work begins.

PC Emporium is not responsible for data loss during repair — customers should back up their data before sending in a device.

Turnaround time for repairs is typically 5-10 business days.
