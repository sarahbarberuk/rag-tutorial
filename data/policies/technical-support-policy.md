# Technical Support Policy

PC Emporium provides free technical support for all products purchased from our store for a period of 12 months from the date of purchase. Support is available via email at support@pcemporium.com and via live chat on the website Monday to Friday, 9am to 6pm EST.

Support covers installation guidance, compatibility questions, and basic troubleshooting.

PC Emporium does not provide support for third-party software, operating system issues unrelated to hardware, or products purchased from other retailers.

For issues beyond the 12-month support period, customers are encouraged to contact the manufacturer directly.
