# Domestic Shipping Policy

PC Emporium ships to all addresses within the United States. Standard shipping is free on all orders over $50. Orders under $50 incur a flat shipping fee of $5.99.

Express shipping (1-2 business days) is available for $14.99. Same-day delivery is available in New York, Los Angeles, Chicago, Houston, and San Francisco for orders placed before 12pm local time, at a cost of $24.99.

Orders are typically dispatched within 1 business day of payment being confirmed. Delivery times are estimates and are not guaranteed. PC Emporium is not liable for delays caused by the carrier.
