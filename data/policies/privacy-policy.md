# Privacy Policy

PC Emporium collects personal data including name, email address, delivery address, and payment information for the purpose of processing orders. This data is stored securely and is not sold to third parties.

PC Emporium may share data with third-party service providers including Stripe (payment processing), Affirm (financing), Klarna, Clearpay, and shipping carriers solely for the purpose of fulfilling orders.

Customers can request a copy of their data or request deletion of their account and associated data at any time by emailing privacy@pcemporium.com.

Data is retained for 7 years in accordance with US financial record-keeping regulations. PC Emporium complies with the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).
