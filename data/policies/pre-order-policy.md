# Pre-Order Policy

PC Emporium offers pre-orders on selected upcoming products. Payment is taken in full at the time of pre-order.

Pre-order prices are guaranteed — if the product launches at a lower price than the pre-order price, customers will be refunded the difference automatically.

Pre-orders can be cancelled at any time before the product ships for a full refund. Estimated dispatch dates are shown on the product page but are subject to change based on manufacturer schedules.

Customers will be notified by email if the dispatch date changes by more than 14 days.
