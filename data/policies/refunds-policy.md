# Refunds Policy

Refunds are issued to the original payment method used at checkout. Credit and debit card refunds typically appear within 5-7 business days depending on the card issuer. PayPal refunds are usually processed within 3-5 business days.

Buy Now Pay Later refunds (Klarna and Clearpay) are processed within 5-10 business days and will reduce the outstanding balance or result in a refund if the balance has already been paid in full.

Shipping costs are non-refundable unless the return is due to a PC Emporium error or a faulty product.

If a partial order was delivered, only the undelivered items will be refunded.
