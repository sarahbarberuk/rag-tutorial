# Recycling & Disposal Policy

PC Emporium offers a free recycling service for old PC components and electronics when purchasing a replacement product. To use this service, select the recycling option at checkout. A prepaid return label will be included with your order for you to send back the old item.

Accepted items include GPUs, CPUs, motherboards, RAM, SSDs, and monitors. Items containing hazardous materials such as CRT monitors or batteries must be disposed of in accordance with local regulations and cannot be sent via standard post.

PC Emporium partners with a certified e-waste recycling facility to ensure all items are disposed of responsibly. Data destruction certificates are available on request for SSDs and hard drives.
