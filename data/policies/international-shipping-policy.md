# International Shipping Policy

PC Emporium ships to Canada, the United Kingdom, Australia, and most European Union countries. International shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on the destination country and the weight of the order.

Estimated delivery times are 7-14 business days for Canada and the UK, and 10-21 business days for Australia and EU countries.

Customers are responsible for any customs duties, import taxes, or local fees charged by their country. PC Emporium is not responsible for delays caused by customs processing.

International orders are not eligible for same-day or express shipping. Some products, including large monitors and pre-built PCs, cannot be shipped internationally due to size and weight restrictions.
