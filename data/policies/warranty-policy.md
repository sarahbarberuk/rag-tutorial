# Warranty Policy

All products sold by PC Emporium include the manufacturer's standard warranty. GPUs carry a 3-year manufacturer warranty. CPUs carry a 3-year manufacturer warranty. Motherboards carry a 3-year manufacturer warranty. RAM carries a lifetime warranty. SSDs carry a 5-year manufacturer warranty. Monitors carry a 3-year manufacturer warranty.

Warranty claims must be submitted within the warranty period and require proof of purchase. To make a warranty claim, contact support@pcemporium.com with your order number, a description of the fault, and photos or video evidence of the issue where possible.

PC Emporium will liaise with the manufacturer on the customer's behalf.

Warranty does not cover physical damage, liquid damage, or faults caused by incorrect installation.
