# Returns Policy (Updated March 2021)

In response to the ongoing COVID-19 pandemic and the disruption to postal services and daily life, PC Emporium has temporarily updated its returns policy to give customers more flexibility.

## Returns window

We have extended our standard returns window from 30 days to 60 days for all orders placed between March 2021 and further notice. We understand that many customers are unable to visit post offices or arrange collections during this period and we want to make sure nobody is left out of pocket.

## Condition of returned items

We have also relaxed our usual requirement that items be returned in unused, resalable condition. During the pandemic period, we will accept returns on opened items provided the product is complete and undamaged.

## Return shipping

PC Emporium will cover the cost of return shipping on all returns during the pandemic period, regardless of the reason for return. Customers will receive a prepaid return label by email within 1 business day of submitting a return request.

## How to initiate a return

To initiate a return, email support@pcemporium.com with your order number and a brief description of the reason for your return. No further documentation is required during this period.

## Refunds

Refunds will be processed within 3-5 business days of receiving the returned item and will be issued to the original payment method.

## Custom-built PCs

Custom-built PCs are also eligible for return during the pandemic period, provided they are returned in the original packaging and no modifications have been made after delivery.
