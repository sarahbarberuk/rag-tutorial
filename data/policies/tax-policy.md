# Tax Policy

PC Emporium charges sales tax in accordance with US state tax laws. Sales tax is calculated at checkout based on the delivery address.

Customers in states with no sales tax (e.g. Oregon, Montana, New Hampshire, Delaware, and Alaska) will not be charged sales tax.

Business customers with a valid tax exemption certificate can apply for tax-exempt status by emailing support@pcemporium.com with their certificate before placing an order. Tax exemptions cannot be applied retrospectively to completed orders.
