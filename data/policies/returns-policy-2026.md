# Returns Policy

PC Emporium accepts returns within 30 days of delivery for most products. Items must be returned in their original packaging and in an unused, resalable condition.

To initiate a return, customers must contact support@pcemporium.com with their order number and reason for return. Once the return is approved, customers will receive a prepaid return shipping label by email within 2 business days.

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days of receiving the returned item and will be issued to the original payment method.

Items that have been opened and used are not eligible for a standard return but may be eligible for a warranty claim.

Custom-built PCs and special-order items are non-returnable unless they arrive faulty.
