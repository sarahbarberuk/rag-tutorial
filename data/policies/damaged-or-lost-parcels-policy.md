# Damaged or Lost Parcels Policy

If your order arrives damaged, please do not discard the packaging. Take photos of the damaged packaging and the product and email them to support@pcemporium.com within 48 hours of delivery along with your order number. PC Emporium will arrange a replacement or refund at no cost to the customer.

If your order is marked as delivered by the carrier but has not arrived, please wait 24 hours before contacting us as parcels are sometimes scanned as delivered before they are physically dropped off.

If the parcel has still not arrived after 24 hours, contact support@pcemporium.com and we will open an investigation with the carrier. Lost parcel investigations typically take 5-10 business days. If the parcel is confirmed lost, a replacement or full refund will be issued.
