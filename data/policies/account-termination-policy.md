# Account Termination Policy

Customers can close their PC Emporium account at any time by emailing support@pcemporium.com. Accounts with open orders, active warranties, or outstanding refunds cannot be closed until those are resolved.

PC Emporium reserves the right to suspend or permanently terminate accounts that are found to be engaged in fraudulent activity, abuse of the returns or price match policy, or violation of the terms of service.

Terminated accounts will not be eligible to open a new account. Data associated with terminated accounts is retained for 7 years in accordance with financial regulations.
