# Dead on Arrival (DOA) Policy

If a product arrives faulty or does not function as expected straight out of the box, it is considered Dead on Arrival (DOA).

DOA claims must be reported to PC Emporium within 7 days of delivery by emailing support@pcemporium.com with your order number and a description of the fault. Where possible, please include photos or video evidence.

DOA products will be replaced with an identical item at no cost to the customer, including return shipping. If the product is out of stock, a full refund will be issued.

DOA claims reported after 7 days will be handled under the standard warranty policy.
