# Price Match Policy

PC Emporium will match the price of any identical product sold by an authorised US retailer, provided the competitor's price is publicly listed and the item is in stock at the time of the request.

Price match requests can be submitted before or within 7 days of purchase by emailing support@pcemporium.com with a link to the competitor's listing.

Price matches are not available on marketplace listings (e.g. Amazon third-party sellers, eBay), clearance or liquidation sales, bundle deals, or prices that include membership discounts (e.g. Amazon Prime). Price matches are also not available on pre-orders or backorders.

Only one price match per order is permitted.
