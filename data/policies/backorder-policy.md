# Backorder Policy

Some products on PC Emporium may be listed as available for backorder when they are temporarily out of stock. Backordered items can be purchased at the current listed price. Estimated restock dates are shown on the product page where available.

If an estimated date is not available, customers will be notified by email when the item is back in stock and ready to dispatch.

Customers can cancel a backorder at any time before dispatch for a full refund. PC Emporium reserves the right to cancel a backorder if a product is discontinued or if the restock is delayed beyond 90 days, in which case a full refund will be issued automatically.
