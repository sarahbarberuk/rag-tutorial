# Payment Methods Policy

PC Emporium accepts Visa, Mastercard, American Express, and Discover credit and debit cards. PayPal and Apple Pay are also accepted.

Buy Now Pay Later options are available through Klarna (Pay in 3, Pay in 30 days) and Clearpay (Pay in 4).

Cryptocurrency payments are not accepted. All transactions are processed in USD.

PC Emporium uses Stripe as its payment processor and does not store card details. Payments are encrypted using TLS 1.3.
